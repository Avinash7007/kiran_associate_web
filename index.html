About Kiran Associate

A trusted partner for every stage of your compliance journey

Kiran Associate is a professionally managed Chartered Accountancy firm dedicated to providing comprehensive taxation, accounting, regulatory compliance, audit and business advisory services to individuals, startups, MSMEs and corporate clients across India.
